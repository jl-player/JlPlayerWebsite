Delete Your JL Player Account

If you would like to delete your JL Player account and associated data, you can follow the steps below.

Option 1: Delete from App

  1. Open the JL Player app
  2. Go to Private Folder / Locker
  3. Reset or remove your password (if available)

This will remove access to your secured data within the app.

Option 2: Request via Email

You can also request account deletion by sending an email:

📧 Email: enquiry.jlplayer@gmail.com

📝 Subject: Delete My Account

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your email address
  • Stored account credentials
  • Locker-related data

Data Retention

Some data may be retained temporarily for security or legal purposes. This data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
