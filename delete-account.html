To request deletion of your JL Player account and associated data: 1. Open the JL Player app 2. Go to Private Folder / Locker 3. Reset or remove your password (if available) OR Send an email request to: developers.jlplayer@gmail.com Subject: Delete My Account We will delete: - Your email address - Stored account credentials - Locker-related data Retention: Some data may be retained temporarily for security or legal purposes (up to 30 days), after which it will be permanently deleted.
